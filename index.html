Understanding Personality Traits, Needs, and Values

GREEN

The Conceptual Thinker: Logical, Analytical, Innovative

Core Needs

  • Intellectual stimulation & challenges
  • Autonomy & creative freedom
  • Time to ponder & question
  • Privacy & information
  • Competency & innovation
How to Interact: Be rational, precise, objective. Validate their ideas.

ORANGE

The Dynamic Doer: Spontaneous, Energetic, Action-Oriented

Core Needs

  • Freedom & flexibility
  • Immediate results (expediency)
  • Recognition for skillfulness
  • Variety & physical activity
  • To be given options
How to Interact: Be direct, confident, entertaining. Avoid routine.

BLUE

The Compassionate Connector: Empathetic, Sincere, Authentic

Core Needs

  • Acceptance & validation
  • Harmony & empathy
  • Self-expression
  • Authentic connection
  • To contribute & be understood
How to Interact: Be caring, open, nice. Focus on the human element.

GOLD

The Responsible Organizer: Dependable, Structured, Traditional

Core Needs

  • Consistency & reliability
  • Clear expectations & rules
  • Structure & organization
  • Responsibility & closure
  • Respect for time
How to Interact: Be accurate, thorough, consistent. Respect traditions.
